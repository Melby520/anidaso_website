GOVERNANCE

Anidaso Family Protection Fund is governed with care, responsibility, and integrity. Our governance structure ensures that decisions are made fairly, processes are clear, and the interests of members and families are always placed first.



TRUST

  • Our Commitment to Trust
  • Managed by a dedicated committee
  • Anidaso is overseen by a committed group of individuals responsible for governance, oversight, and the fair administration of the Fund.
  • Clear membership rules

    Membership terms, responsibilities, and eligibility criteria are clearly defined to ensure consistency, fairness, and understanding for all members.

  • Transparent processes

    From registration to community contributions and support payments, our processes are documented, communicated, and applied openly.

  • Community accountability

    Anidaso is accountable to the community it serves. Decisions are guided by shared values, ethical standards, and the long-term sustainability of the Fund.
