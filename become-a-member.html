BECOME A MEMBER

Becoming an active part of a shared responsibility

Once registration is completed, you officially become an Anidaso member by paying the required registration fee and activating your membership.

As an Anidaso member, you:

  • Join a community built on unity, compassion, and shared responsibility
  • Agree to make regular contributions to support the sustainability of the fund
  • Have the ability to nominate and update your next of kin
  • Are expected to comply with Anidaso’s membership rules and governance processes

Membership is open to individuals and families of all ages and backgrounds. Being a member means standing together with others to ensure that no family faces loss alone.
