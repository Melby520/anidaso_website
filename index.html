WHO WE ARE

Anidaso Family Protection Fund is a community-based initiative founded on unity, compassion, and shared responsibility.

We bring individuals and families together to support one another during life's most difficult moments. Through collective participation, Anidaso provides a structured way for communities to stand together and ensure that families are not left to face loss alone.

Our approach is grounded in dignity, transparency, and mutual care, reflecting the values of the communities we serve.

Why Join Anidaso

Joining Anidaso means becoming part of a trusted community built on shared responsibility and mutual care. Our model is simple, transparent, and rooted in the belief that families are strongest when they stand together.

UNITED KINGDOM - MEMBERSHIP STRUCTURE

Membership Contributions

  • £50 one-off registration fee
    Payable at the point of joining Anidaso UK.
  • £25 monthly contribution
    Paid by active members to sustain the UK community fund.

Community Support Payment

  • £6,000 community support payment
    Payable to the deceased member’s nominated next of kin, subject to:
  • A minimum 6 months of active membership
  • Up-to-date monthly contributions
  • Compliance with Anidaso UK membership terms
  • Governance and fund sustainability checks at the time of claim

GHANA - MEMBERSHIP STRUCTURE

Membership Contributions

  • GHS 200 one-off registration fee
    Payable at the point of joining Anidaso Ghana.
  • GHS 50 monthly contribution
    Paid by active members to sustain the Ghana community fund.

Community Support Payment

  • GHS 20,000 community support payment
    Payable to the deceased member’s nominated next of kin, subject to:
  • A minimum 6 months of active membership
  • Up-to-date monthly contributions
  • Compliance with Anidaso Ghana membership terms
  • Governance and fund sustainability checks at the time of claim

GOVERNANCE

Anidaso Family Protection Fund is governed with care, responsibility, and integrity. Our governance structure ensures that decisions are made fairly, processes are clear, and the interests of members and families are always placed first.

TRUST

  • Our Commitment to Trust
  • Managed by a dedicated committee
  • Anidaso is overseen by a committed group of individuals responsible for governance, oversight, and the fair administration of the Fund.
  • Clear membership rules

    Membership terms, responsibilities, and eligibility criteria are clearly defined to ensure consistency, fairness, and understanding for all members.

  • Transparent processes

    From registration to community contributions and support payments, our processes are documented, communicated, and applied openly.

  • Community accountability

    Anidaso is accountable to the community it serves. Decisions are guided by shared values, ethical standards, and the long-term sustainability of the Fund.
