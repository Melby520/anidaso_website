REGISTER

Your first step into the Anidaso community

Registering with Anidaso allows you to formally express your interest in joining a trusted, community-led family protection initiative. The registration process is designed to be simple, respectful, and secure.

When you register, you will be asked to:

  • Provide your basic personal details
  • Confirm your country of membership (UK or Ghana)
  • Nominate a next of kin who would receive support if needed
  • Review and accept Anidaso’s constitution, membership rules, and values

Registration helps Anidaso maintain accurate records, uphold transparency, and ensure that all members are treated fairly and consistently. Registration alone does not guarantee membership until the process is completed.

For more information or to complete registration, please contact the Anidaso team or use the membership form on the website.
