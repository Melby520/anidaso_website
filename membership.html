What Sets Anidaso Apart

Community-led

Anidaso is shaped and sustained by the people it serves, ensuring decisions are made with fairness and collective wellbeing at the centre.

Affordable participation

A modest registration fee and small community contributions make participation accessible without placing undue financial burden on members

Transparent structure

Clear membership rules, defined processes, and accountable governance provide confidence and clarity at every stage.

Built for families

Designed to support individuals and families of all ages, with provisions for parents or guardians to register on behalf of children.
