WHO WE ARE

Anidaso Family Protection Fund is a community-based initiative founded on unity, compassion, and shared responsibility.

We bring individuals and families together to support one another during life's most difficult moments. Through collective participation, Anidaso provides a structured way for communities to stand together and ensure that families are not left to face loss alone.

Our approach is grounded in dignity, transparency, and mutual care, reflecting the values of the communities we serve.
