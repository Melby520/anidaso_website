COMMUNITY SUPPORT

Support when it matters most

In the event of a member’s death, Anidaso may provide a community support payment to the member’s nominated next of kin, subject to eligibility and governance review.

Community support:

  • Is intended to assist families during a difficult and emotional time
  • Is provided in a dignified and respectful manner
  • Is subject to a minimum active membership period
  • Requires verification and review by the governing committee
  • Is dependent on fund sustainability at the time of the request

Anidaso operates as a community-led family protection fund and not an insurance scheme. Community support payments are discretionary and are not guaranteed. This approach helps protect the long-term stability of the fund and the interests of all members.
