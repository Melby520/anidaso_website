MONTHLY CONTRIBUTION

A shared commitment that sustains the community

Monthly contributions are a core part of how Anidaso operates. These contributions allow the fund to remain sustainable and able to support families during times of loss.

Monthly contributions:

  • Are affordable and clearly defined based on your country of membership
  • Are collected and recorded transparently
  • Help build and maintain the strength of the community fund
  • Reflect a shared commitment rather than a commercial transaction

Regular contributions ensure that Anidaso can continue to operate responsibly and fairly for all members. Keeping contributions up to date is essential for maintaining active membership status.
